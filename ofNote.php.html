  • People Of Note

  • Col. Reginald Vanderbush III, Esq.
    The Mórríghan Patron is the sort of individual who funds grand schemes and ludicrous adventures with the hopes of finding impossible treasures. He is elusive and typically unavailable, but spends most of his time in New Orleans gambling. No one really knows where his apparent bottomless wealth comes from, but rumor has it his "Aunt Vickie" is fond of her nephew and sends financial gifts from England.
  • Avery Ames
    The story of STEAM is told from the perspective of Avery Ames, our trusty narrator. No one is quite certain who he really is--is he a reporter, a character of the Mórríghan, or something...more sinister?
  • The Lady In Red
    A woman of wealth and worldly pleasures who keeps mysteriously appearing in the crew's line of sight.
