  • The Villains

  • Persephone crew:
  • Col. Beauregard Bartholameus White:

    Captain of the Persephone. He is a strong southern man, his family owned a plantation and he believes in freedom for all-white men. He hates Captain Dez, but he is typically the utmost gentleman in her presence because he is the picture of a chauvinist. Rather abusive of his crew. Has the Persephone because he stole it, and is constantly hiring new crew members to improve its battlements. He finds ways to "show up" unannounced at the worst possible moments.

  • The Great Wizard:
    According to the Professor, the Wizard is a sham and a con artist who practices the arcane art of hypnotism. His appearance in Episode 3 causes the crew some great discomfort, but they have their own problems to worry about.
